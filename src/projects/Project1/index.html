EdgeBook

EdgeBook

A comprehensive online trading journal that converts user trade logs into visual insights—performance charts,
strategy analytics, profitability trends, and psychological patterns—allowing traders to
make smarter decisions and continuously refine their edge.

Problem

Successful trading depends on consistent tracking and evaluation. Even basic trade data can provide powerful insights that improve future decision-making. While spreadsheets can store information, they require too much manual work, making it difficult and time-consuming for traders to convert data into meaningful conclusions.

Solution

Develop a web-based trading journal platform that automatically transforms trade inputs into actionable analytics. The system will calculate:

Strategy win rates

Best performing days and times

Common trading mistakes

Probability of long-term profitability

Psychological influences on outcomes

To reduce emotional, impulsive, or low-quality trades, the platform also includes a Trading Session module that forces traders to validate each trade using a checklist before executing it.

Key Features

Performance Analytics & Charts

All journal entries are converted into visual insights, including:

Daily, weekly, monthly, and all-time metrics
Profit and loss breakdowns
Equity growth analysis
Strategy-based win rate and performance trends

Session-Based Trading Discipline

To prevent emotional or impulsive decisions, traders operate within “sessions.” During each session, the trader completes:

Strategy checklist validation before taking a trade
Emotional/psychological self-assessment before and after trading
Trade evaluation after closing the position

Strategy Management

Users can create and manage multiple strategies, each with its own rule-based checklist. This module helps traders:

Ensure every trade follows a defined rule set
Identify rule violations leading to losses
Track which strategy performs best

Trade Management

All trades submitted are organized and managed within the system, including:

Completed trades
Open or active trades
Ongoing performance evaluation

Traders can filter, tag, and evaluate trades anytime.

Account Management

Users can register multiple trading accounts and monitor:

Individual account performance
Combined portfolio performance
Growth, consistency, and risk exposure

Overall Value

This trading journal turns raw trade logs into automated insights, psychological awareness, and rule-based discipline, helping traders:

Reduce emotional trading

Improve long-term profitability

Master strategy execution

Track performance effortlessly

It is not just a journal— it becomes a decision-making system for continuous improvement.
